o Discussion & Q&A
- Marko: Expressed difficulty in understanding the distinction between regular network functions and I2INF.

- Dr. Yang: Sought clarification on whether the project is vehicle-related and requested a reference for the interface.
Highlighted that the presence of the compute domain interface distinguishes I2INF from I2NSF.

- Kehan Yao:
1. Emphasized the necessity of GPU parameter aggregation for AI learning, with the compute domain interface essential for acceleration.
2. Shared AD’s reflection—collaboration with operations/companies is essential.
3. Net computing is challenging in academia and clarification is needed due to close similarity with CATS WG.
4. There is demand for compute domain vs. network domain configuration; compute side configuration is an area of concern outside the network domain.

- Dr. Lee: Noted potential overlap with NMRG scope and highlighted the need to clarify roles between working groups and research groups.
